August 6-10, 2012 - Surf 4 Kidz Summer Camp

One of our favorite annual events, the Surf 4 Kidz summer camp combines a charitable fundraiser with the opportunity to learn how to surf! Camp founder Paul Leach conducts this camp each year along with a very special closing ceremony. All of the proceeds go to The Sitting Tree's annual Thanksgiving grocery event that helps hundreds of people enjoy a Thanksgiving meal they would not have otherwise. Big thanks to Paul for all his hard work!
